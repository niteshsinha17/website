IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) GBPEC

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) GBPEC is the Student Branch WIE Affinity Group of G.B. Pant Engineering College under Delhi Section and R10 region. We as an affinity group, strive for enhancing women participation in STEM fields and instill leadership roles for women. IEEE WIE-GBPEC intends to create a budding stage for women across the globe to network, enhance knowledge base and achieve breakthroughs in their STEM careers. WIE-GBPEC was started in 2019 and has been active in organizing seminars, workshops and technical events throughout the year.
